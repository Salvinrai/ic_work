About

An adaptable and sustainable way to bridge the communication gap between specially abled and abled people

Our product will create a bridge among online and off-line median which will transmit the fetched data to multiple users through Wi-Fi direct at a time.
The product will allow mobile phones to connect with bridge adapter without internet connection through Wifi within a radius of 100 metres.
Mentors will send data to Organisations. They will further filter and classify the data that is it reliable or not for posting. After classification, Data will be uploaded to panel though internet.
